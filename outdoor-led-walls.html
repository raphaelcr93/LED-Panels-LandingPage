Outdoor LED walls, designed for Australian residential spaces.

What it is

A screen wall built from LED modules.

Outdoor LED walls are built from multiple LED panels that work together as one large screen. For residential use, the goal is not advertising or event hire. The goal is a permanent outdoor entertainment feature that feels integrated with the home.

The right specification depends on the viewing distance, screen size, pixel pitch, brightness, controller, mounting structure, power, access and exposure. Those details matter more than a simple size comparison with a television.

Where it fits

Designed around outdoor living, not shopfront signage.

Patios and pergolas

For covered or semi-covered entertaining spaces where the screen is visible from outdoor seating.

Pool areas

For open-air cinema nights, sport events and summer gatherings, subject to electrical and splash-zone planning.

Alfresco kitchens

For homes where the outdoor kitchen, BBQ and screen become one social zone.

Luxury projects

For builders, landscapers and owners considering an architectural entertainment feature.

Specification

The premium result comes from the planning.

OpenAir Pixels is validating demand before launch. The following items would need to be reviewed before a real project proceeds.

01Screen position

Viewing angle, sun direction, seating, neighbours and the architectural line of the space.

02Hardware selection

Outdoor-rated LED hardware, pixel pitch, controller, brightness and service access.

03Structure and power

Mounting, wind exposure, electrical requirements, drainage and safe placement near pools.

04Compliance review

Local requirements may vary based on size, structure, brightness, use and location.
