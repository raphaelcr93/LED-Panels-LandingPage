Experience

For outdoor rooms that are already the centre of the home.

The Poolside Cinema Wall concept is for homeowners who want a larger, brighter and more immersive entertainment feature than a standard outdoor TV or projector setup.

It can suit pool areas, alfresco kitchens and covered outdoor rooms, depending on the selected hardware, site exposure, power, structure and viewing position.

Poolside planning

Near-water installations need a careful project review.

Electrical safety

Any poolside project needs qualified electrical review and safe placement around splash zones.

Weather exposure

Outdoor-rated hardware can be specified, but exposure, drainage and structure still matter.

Glare and sun path

Brightness and daytime viewing depend on hardware, screen position and the direction of sunlight.

Viewing comfort

The screen should work from outdoor seating, pool edges and social zones without dominating the home.
