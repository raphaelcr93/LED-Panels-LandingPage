Premium fit

For properties where the outdoor space is part of the value.

The Architectural LED Feature Wall concept is for higher-end projects where the screen should feel designed into the environment, not added as an afterthought.

It may suit luxury homes, architect-led renovations, landscape projects, boutique accommodation and short-stay properties where the entertainment area helps create a memorable guest experience.

Design lens

The screen should feel intentional.

01Architectural integration

Screen proportions, framing, backing structure and surrounding finishes need to work with the home.
