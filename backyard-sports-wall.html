Made for

Patios, pergolas, BBQ areas and family homes.

The Backyard Sports Wall concept is for the main outdoor living area: a fixed screen position that can be enjoyed from the table, lounge, BBQ area or pool edge.

It is not intended to feel like a venue screen dropped into a home. The screen position, framing and size should feel connected to the backyard layout and the way people naturally gather.
