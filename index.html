Outdoor LED walls

A bigger backyard screen without the projector setup.

An outdoor LED wall is made from modular LED panels that work together as one large screen. For a home, the point is practical: a bigger outdoor display for sport, movies and gatherings.

OpenAir Pixels is shaping simple residential packages with outdoor-rated panels, a dark metallic frame, controller setup and optional or included outdoor audio depending on the package. Final specifications are subject to site review and selected hardware.

Australian backyard lifestyle

Made for sport nights, summer movies and casual gaming.

Put the screen where people already gather. Watch NRL, AFL, cricket, State of Origin, F1, UFC or movie nights from the patio, pool edge or BBQ area.

Gaming can also be part of the use case, especially casual games, racing games, sport games and party games. Serious competitive gaming needs latency reviewed during specification.
