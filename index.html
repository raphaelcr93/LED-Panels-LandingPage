The opportunity

Bigger open-air entertainment without making the backyard feel commercial.

Outdoor TVs and projectors can be limited by size, brightness, glare, placement and weather conditions. A custom outdoor LED wall can create a larger, more immersive entertainment experience for homeowners who want their alfresco space to feel considered, modern and memorable.

Every project would be designed around the selected hardware and actual site conditions. Screen brightness, pixel pitch, structure, access, electrical work, weather exposure and viewing distance all need to be reviewed before installation.

Why LED

A custom LED wall is a different kind of backyard upgrade.

Compared with outdoor TVs

  • Larger custom screen sizes
  • Better suited to big backyard viewing experiences
  • More architectural than a standard TV
  • Premium wow-factor for entertaining spaces

Compared with projectors

  • Can be specified for brighter outdoor conditions
  • Designed for fixed outdoor entertaining zones
  • No need for a projection surface in the middle of the space
  • Performance depends on screen position, sun direction and hardware

Compared with commercial LED screens

  • Residential-first design intent
  • Specified around patios, pools and alfresco living
  • Modular servicing and repairability
  • Designed as part of an outdoor entertaining space

FAQ

Questions homeowners ask first.

Is this a TV?

Not a standard TV. It is a modular LED screen wall designed for large-format outdoor entertainment.

Can it be used during the day?

LED walls can be specified for brighter environments, but final performance depends on selected hardware, screen position, sun direction and installation design.

Is it weatherproof?

Outdoor-rated LED hardware can be specified, but the final setup must consider exposure, drainage, structure, electrical work and installation conditions.

How much does it cost?

Early validation ranges start from approx. $15k-$25k installed for a Backyard Sports Wall, $25k-$50k for a Poolside Cinema Wall, and usually $50k+ for architectural custom projects. Pricing depends on size, pixel pitch, structure, electrical work, access and installation.

Can it be installed near a pool?

Poolside installations may be possible, but they need careful planning around electrical safety, splash zones, structure and viewing position.

Do I need council approval?

Requirements may vary depending on location, structure, size, brightness, use and local rules. Customers should check local requirements before installation.
